KERO KERO BONITO

Photo of Kero Kero Bonito performing in Brighton, 2018, by Questioner2233 [CC0] on Wikimedia Commons.

Kero Kero Bonito (KKB) is a British indie band from London, England. The band consists of vocalist Sarah Midori Perry and producers Gus Lobban & Jamie Bulled. The name Kero Kero Bonito are Japanese onomatopoeic words for frog croaks and a type of fish. Kero Kero Bonito draws inspiration from Japanese pop culture and video game music which shows in their fun and bubbly tunes.
